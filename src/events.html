The Elite Series events represent the best level. Those are the biggest events of the year with players that made the history of Trackmania. Every Elite Series event is played offline in prestigious venues such as Gamer's Assembly or DreamHack. They are the main events to focus on if you want to qualify to the GRS All-Star event.

Ascend Series Events

The Ascend Series is the perfect place for future talents. There are more accessible offline events as well as big online events. This mix allows the players to compete for the win but also to challenge the best players in the world.

Entry Series events

The Entry Series is built for the new players that want to discover the competitive scene. Those events are the best way to meet new people while exploring the Global Racing Series.
