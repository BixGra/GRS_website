GRS All-Star

The GRS All-Star is the final event of the season where he best players in the GRS Ranking and winners of the Open Qualifier fight for a prestigious title.

Presentation

At the end of the year, the best players will compete in head-to-head matches to be crowned GRS Champion. Even if the format may evolve, the main goals are to have the elite of Trackmania compete together, to celebrate a year of events and to highlight what the community has to offer during the year.

GRS All-Star format

The GRS All-Star format of can change depending on the support given to the Global Racing Series. Indeed, the event starts as an online competition, but it can really quickly grow to an offline or even on-stage event!

Level 0, starter level - Validated

This is the minimal version or the GRS All-Star. The best 8 players of the season will compete in an online bracket in order to win the title. In this form, the GRS All-Star will be cast by one talent and have a minimal cash prize.

Level 1, online finals - Pending

The event is still online, but we now have two casters and, most importantly, we are starting to collect funds for the 2025 Elite Series events.

Level 2, offline casters - Pending

The casters will be on a live set with a production team, a desk and maybe some additional content. This means that they cast solely focus on what they do best, thrilling us! We are also still collecting funds for 2025 Elite Series events.

Level 3, offline finals - Pending

This is the main goal, the players and the caster are on the live set with, unfortunately, no audience. This is becoming serious and the production also increase it's quality. Players expenses will be, at least partly, covered and the cash prize is shared in a way that will allow everyone to enjoy the event while competing for the title. Of course, we are still collecting fund for 2025 Elite Series events.

Level 4, TM Awards - Pending

With our media partner TM News, we are able to host the 2024 TM Awards on the live set. More than just a competition, the GRS All-Star is a show with events announcements, mapping showcases and fun side-events. In addition, we are still collecting funds for 2025 Elite Series events, but we are also starting to collect funds for 2025 Ascend Series events!

Level 5, on-stage with spectators - Pending

Of course, the show is even bette with spectators, but it adds some costs. That's why this only comes with the level 5. We want the maximum amount of people to enjoy this event but there won't be many seats available! We are still collecting funds for 2025 Elite Series and Ascend Series events.

Level 6, 2 days event - Pending

If things become crazy, well, let's have 2 days of competition and even more content. Obviously, we are still collecting funds for 2025 Elite Series and Ascend Series events.
