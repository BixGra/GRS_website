GRS Ranking calculation and rules

Discover how the Global Racing Series Ranking is calculated. You'll find here the formats, point repartition and calculation system for the GRS Ranking.

Overview

The Global Racing Series Ranking takes into account the last 365 days of competition. It is updated every 1st and 15th of each month.

The only exception being if an Elite Series event takes place on the same day. In that case, the ranking will be updated on the first day after the end of the event.

The events are split into three series:
- The Elite Series gathering the bigger offline events and the best players in high stakes tournaments.
- The Ascend Series where the future talents can express themselves and try to challenge the best players in smaller offline events or in the bigger online tournaments.
- The Entry Series for the new players that want to discover the Global Racing Series events and begin their journey to the top of the GMS Ranking.

Elite Series

The Elite Series events represent the best level. Those are the biggest events of the year with players that made the history of Trackmania. Every Elite Series event is played offline in prestigious venues such as Gamer's Assembly or DreamHack. They are the main events to focus on if you want to qualify to the GRS All-Star event.
