Events

The Global Racing Series is divided into 3 series, each one corresponding to an approach of the competition. The Elite Series for the best players, the Ascend Series for the future talents and the Entry Series for the new players. Discover those 3 series in this section.

GRS All-Star

The GRS All-Star is the final event of the season. The best players in the GRS Ranking and the winners of the Open Qualifier are invited and fight for a prestigious title.

Elite Series events

The Elite Series events represent the best level. Those are the biggest events of the year with players that made the history of Trackmania. Every Elite Series event is played offline in prestigious venues such as Gamer's Assembly or ComicCon. They are the main events to focus on if you want to qualify to the GRS All-Star event.

Ascend Series events

The Ascend Series is the perfect place for future talents. There are more accessible offline events as well as big online events. This mix allows the players to compete for the win but also to challenge the best players in the world.

Entry Series events

The Entry Series is built for the new players that want to discover the competitive scene. Those online events are the best way to meet new people while exploring the Global Racing Series.
