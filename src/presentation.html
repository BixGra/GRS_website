The project

The Global Racing Series is a circuit of events held on the racing game Trackmania. This circuit gathers 25 events ranging from the beginner-friendly fun tournament to the top-end competition between the best players in the world. All along the year, the players earn points by participating in any event of the circuit.

The season ends with the GRS All-Star where the best players fight for a prestigious title. Keep in touch, the format of the event can change depending on the support given to the Global Racing Series. Indeed, the event starts as an online competition, but it can really quickly grow to an offline or even on-stage event!

Organizing the GRS All-Star involves a lot of different expenses. The main fields to take into account are the design/graphics, the casters/talents, the cash-prize, the production/venue (if the events takes place offline) but not only.

Since the GRS is build with the events, we also want to help the organizers finance their project. This means that at least 10% of the 2024 total GRS budget is allocated to help finance 2025 partner events.
