Elite Series events

The Elite Series events represent the best level. Those are the biggest events of the year with players that made the history of Trackmania. Every Elite Series event is played offline in prestigious venues such as Gamer's Assembly or TBA. They are the main events to focus on if you want to qualify to the GRS All-Star.
