GRS All-Star

The GRS All-Star is the final event of the season where he best players in the GRS Ranking and winners of the Open Qualifier fight for a prestigious title. Keep in touch, the format of the event can change depending on the support given to the Global Racing Series. Indeed, the event starts as an online competition, but it can really quickly grow to an offline or even on-stage even!
